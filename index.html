ABOUT
TRADES

S.A Trades is a trusted retail shop based in Madarpur, Siwan, Bihar, specialising in electrical items, hardware tools, and electrical accessories. We serve homeowners, contractors, electricians, and builders who demand quality products at fair prices.

Founded with a commitment to excellence and genuine products, Trades has become a reliable name in the local market. Whether you need a complete electrical setup for a new home or a single tool for a quick repair, we have you covered.

We source from trusted brands and manufacturers to ensure that every product we sell meets safety standards and delivers long-lasting performance. Our knowledgeable team is always ready to guide you to the right product.
