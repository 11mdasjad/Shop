ABOUT
TRADES

S.A Trades is a trusted retail shop based in Madarpur, Siwan, Bihar, specialising in electrical items, hardware tools, electrical accessories, plumbing supplies, and paints & finishes. We serve homeowners, contractors, electricians, and builders who demand quality products at fair prices.

Founded with a commitment to excellence and genuine products, S.A Trades has become a reliable name in the local market. Whether you need a complete electrical setup for a new home, plumbing for a renovation, or paint for a fresh finish — we have you covered.

We source from trusted brands and manufacturers to ensure that every product we sell meets safety standards and delivers long-lasting performance. Our knowledgeable team is always ready to guide you to the right product.
